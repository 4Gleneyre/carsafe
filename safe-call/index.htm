Safe Call

Safe Call…the Voice of the Future Hear Today!

Distracted driving is the daily number one cause of harm and death worldwide!

ASF is alerting banks and businesses about the use of dangerous and obsolete public telecom that encourages distracted driving with phone prompt requests (akin to requesting text messaging) instead of employing safe and modern voice command technology. Outdated public telecom requires Safe Call upgrade, see, Video. (Please observe fair market research compensation usage terms, and send tax-deductible payment to the address below.) Today, it is important to note that automakers are equipping new vehicles with hands-free speech technology, such as Google Android Auto or Apple CarPlay (or hands-free equipment is being brought on board) to help safely address mobile phone use while driving. These new systems, along with home/office systems such as Amazon Echo, or Google Home, will not function with businesses using outdated public telecom making the requirement for Safe Call technology all the more urgent. Upgrade your business telecom now, and join the ranks of Microsoft, Apple, Amtrak, United Airlines, American Airlines, Delta Airlines, Sheraton Hotels, Capital One, Discovery, Alliant Credit Union, Progressive Insurance, Farmers Insurance, Geico, Allstate, Liberty Mutual, American Family Insurance, BMI, California DMV, and the many other socially responsible, and modern businesses, that now employ voice command telecom, thusly establishing international standards and practices precedent, and the winning argument for the observance of Safe Call recommendations by all businesses with public telecom.
