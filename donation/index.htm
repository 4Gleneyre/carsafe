ASF Global Research and Communications Center

The new GRCC will broadcast the ASF lifesaving message at www.carsafe.org worldwide, while also researching new ways to improve driving safety.

The first GRCC is budgeted at around $1,000,000 with future plans for more centers across the nation.

Your donation will help ASF obtain this new research and communications headquarters as well as fund the WSD, World Safety Drive, and business operations.

ASF can also accept real estate property donations, 1031 donations, and is offering a 10 percent award per the sale of the property to those realtors that referred the donation.

Your donation will help ASF obtain this new research and communications headquarters as well as fund the WSD, World Safety Drive, and business operations. Together we can make a difference! ASF donations are tax deductible.
