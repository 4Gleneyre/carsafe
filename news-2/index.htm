Miscellaneous Driving Tips

Don’t stay in the fast lane for prolonged periods of time so that you can easily pull over to the right shoulder in case of an emergency.

Driving is fraught with danger, minimize your exposure to these dangers by reducing your driving.

Standard equipment side view mirrors have a very hazardous blind spot, and many drivers have yet to obtain blind spot mirror attachments. Therefore, exercise defensive caution when driving in other driver’s blind spots.

Minimize your left turns by trying to increase your right turns (drive around the block if necessary) as left turns are considerably more hazardous.

Be farsighted, look down the road and try to anticipate the rhythm of the stop signals.

Be a considerate driver and avoid creating dangerous situations.
